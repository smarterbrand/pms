Section 8 Punch Lists

A failed inspection is rent you're not collecting. We work Section 8 and HQS punch lists constantly — we fix the findings right the first time and hand you the documentation for re-inspection.

Submit a Work Order Become a Customer

What's included

  • HQS / NSPIRE inspection findings
  • Smoke and CO detector compliance
  • Handrails, guardrails, and trip hazards
  • Window and door deficiencies
  • Peeling paint remediation
  • Re-inspection photo package

How it works for landlords

Send us the inspection report. We turn it into a scope, fix every finding, and return a photo package keyed to the punch list — so the re-inspection is a formality, not a gamble.

Every job runs through our standard five-step process — scoped, approved, dispatched, and closed out with owner-ready documentation.
