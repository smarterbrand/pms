How It Works

A process, not a promise

Every job runs through the same five steps — that's how the work stays fast, consistent, and documented.

Step 1

Submit a work order

Online, by phone, or by email. It's in our system the moment it arrives.

Step 2

We scope & document

We assess, photograph, and build a clear scope with pricing.

Step 3

You approve

You (or your owner) sign off. No surprises, no scope creep.

Step 4

We dispatch & coordinate

Crews scheduled, tenants notified, trades sequenced — handled.

Step 5

Close-out with docs

Before/after photos and a completion report, owner-ready.
